Marco T. Chen

Undergraduate CS @ Tsinghua University

Hi! I’m Marco Chen, a senior CS undergraduate student at Tsinghua University advised by Prof. Jun Zhu and Prof. Jianfei Chen. My current research interests primarily focus on optimization theory and efficient machine learning systems. I am currently a summer visiting researcher at UC Berkeley Sky Computing Lab, advised by Prof. Ion Stoica.

In my free time, I enjoy playing squash, reading, and occasionally playing the violin. If you want to collaborate, play squash, or just want to chat, feel free to email me at marco.ty.chen@gmail.com.
